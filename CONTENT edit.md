# How to Update Your Website

You don't need to know code! Just edit this file and the site updates automatically.

## Quick Steps:

1. **Go to**: https://github.com/YOUR_USERNAME/lema
2. **Click** on `src/data/content.json`
3. **Click** the pencil icon to edit
4. **Make your changes** (see examples below)
5. **Click** "Commit changes"

That's it! The site will update in about a minute.

---

## What to Edit:

### Your Name & Title
```json
"name": "Dr. Jane Smith",
"title": "Associate Professor",
"institution": "Department of Psychology",
```

### Your Tagline
```json
"tagline": "Researching how children learn and develop",
```

### Your Email
```json
"email": "jane.smith@university.edu",
```

### Your Bio
```json
"bio": "Write your full bio here. Tell people about your journey, 
your research, and what makes your work special.",
```

### Publications
```json
{
  "id": 1,
  "title": "Your Paper Title",
  "journal": "Journal Name", 
  "year": "2024",
  "abstract": "Brief summary of the paper...",
  "url": "https://doi.org/..."
}
```

### Community Work
```json
{
  "id": 1,
  "title": "Project Name",
  "description": "What you did for the community",
  "image": "/images/community-1.jpg"
}
```

---

## Adding Photos:

1. Go to https://github.com/YOUR_USERNAME/lema/tree/main/public/images
2. Click "Add file → Upload files"
3. Drag & drop your photos
4. Commit

Then update the JSON to point to your new image (e.g., `/images/my-photo.jpg`)
